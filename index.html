About Our Work

We collaborate with community leaders, shelters, and child-protection agencies in several Asian countries to identify children affected by child labour and provide medical care, education, psychosocial support, and safe housing.

Adoption & Family Support

Adoption is a careful, lawful process. We help families understand international and local adoption laws and guide every step to ensure ethical placements.

  • Step 1: Assessment by local child-protection teams.
  • Step 2: Social and medical evaluations and attempts to reunify with family.
  • Step 3: Legal guardianship or adoption only after full court approval.
